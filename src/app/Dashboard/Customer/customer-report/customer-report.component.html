Frequently Asked Questions

To report a professional, select 'Professional' from the report type dropdown, choose the specific professional, and provide a reason for the report. To report a service, select 'Service' from the report type dropdown, choose the specific service, and provide a reason for the report. After you submit a report, it will be reviewed by our team. If necessary, appropriate actions will be taken based on the report. You can track the status of your report by logging into your account and navigating to the 'Reports' section. There, you will find updates on the progress of your report. Once a report is submitted, you cannot edit it. However, you can withdraw your report by contacting our support team with the report ID. You can report issues related to professionals, services, or packages. This includes inappropriate behavior, poor service quality, or any other concerns you may have.
